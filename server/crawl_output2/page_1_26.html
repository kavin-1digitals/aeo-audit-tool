SENSAI Colours Takes Lip Make‑up to the Next Level

Key Features

  • Finish

MOISTURE INTENSE LIPSTICK

LIPSTICK HOLDER GOLD

Experience next‑level lip make‑up with this lipstick that provides colour‑saturated glow and care in a single glide. Day after day, lips feel less dry and more nourished. The balmy texture melts upon application embracing lips in a supple film to ensure the immaculate finish stays in place.

Available in 12 lavish hues.
Lipstick holder and refillable colours are sold separately.

The Inspiration

Sensory appeal of a traditional confection

The beauty of this lipstick is inspired by Kingyoku, a traditional Japanese confection where colourful designs are captured in thick jelly creating an illusion of depth and enjoyed with their delicate flavours, captivating both the eye and palette.

Carefully formulated, MOISTURE INTENSE LIPSTICK offers the sensory appeal of Kingyoku through its function and finish.

Moisture Intense Formula

Moisture Intense Formula achieves a stunning trifecta of vibrant colour, dewy radiance and comforting moisture, setting a new standard in lip make-up.

The secret behind the function and finish 

Moisture Rich Oil

A blend of select oils achieves a thick, moisturising layer that carries a higher quantity of pigments. Vibrant colour is captured in transparent dew that embraces the lips without compromising moisture.

Cushioning Fixer

A unique combination of polymers contributes as an adhesive agent within the formula to create a soft, malleable film that adheres comfortably with the lips and their movement.

The lipstick that doesn’t compromise care for beauty

Once applied, lips remain moist for 12 hours and resulted in a moisturising effect increased by 68 % compared to before application.* 
Day after day, lip condition looks improved, feeling less dry and more nourished.**

*Based on an instrumental evaluation on 24 women. 
**Based on a one-week self-assessment by 55 women.

Efforts Towards a Beautiful Future

Our commitment to integrating more optimised packaging and ingredients to minimise our environmental impact.

  • Squalane is a highly purified oil extracted from sugarcane.
  • The empty refill can be separated into metal and plastic parts.
  • The lipstick holder is made of at least 15 % of plant-based plastic.
  • The outer carton is made of FSC® certified material.
  • The lipstick holder and refill are sold separately. The lipstick holder can be used more than once by replacing the refill. LIPSTICK HOLDER GOLD can also be used to set the LASTING PLUMP LIPSTICK.

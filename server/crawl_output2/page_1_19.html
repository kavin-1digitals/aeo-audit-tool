Introducing Our New
CLEANSING GEL WATER

Gentle yet efficient
with minimal friction

Friction caused by cleansing can inadvertently damage the skin’s surface. It is generally understood that simply minimising friction can help prevent redness, dryness and other pre-mature signs of skin-ageing.

SENSAI’s new SILKY PURIFYING CLEANSING GEL WATER features Hydro Shift Formula and is designed to reduce friction on the skin’s surface during cleansing.

Saho Double Cleansing

The mindful art of purification

Our unique skincare method grounded in mindful practice, Saho – The Skincare Ritual, is inspired by the code of etiquette from the Japanese tea ceremony that embodies Japan’s spirituality and artistry.

Before entering the tea room, guests are required to wash their hands and cleanse their mouths at a stone wash basin – an etiquette that symbolises the purification of both the body and spirit.

Inspired by this preparatory ritual is Double Cleansing – a two-step routine that begins by removing oil-based impurities such as make-up and sebum, followed by gently lifting away water-based impurities for skin to better receive the treatment that follows.

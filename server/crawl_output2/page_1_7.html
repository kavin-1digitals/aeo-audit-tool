Double Moisturising
The Meticulous Art of
Moisturisation

Double Moisturising is a two-step routine designed to enhance the beautifying benefits of moisturisation. First, a lotion replenishes the skin with a flood of hydration. Then, an emulsion, fluid or cream envelops the skin, sealing in moisture and nourishment. Both steps with their respective roles complement each other to reveal skin’s innate radiance.
